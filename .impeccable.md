## Design Context

### Users
Editorial designers, independent publishers, educators, and technical writers who need browser-native page layout with predictable print output and AI-assisted iteration.

### Brand Personality
Precise, editorial, tool-focused. The product should feel like a serious studio surface: dense enough for repeated professional work, but approachable for prompt-first creation.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light workspace with ink, paper, proofing, and prepress cues. Avoid generic SaaS hero styling and decorative gradients. The first screen is the editor.

### Design Principles
- Make the document the main object, not a marketing illustration.
- Keep controls scan-friendly and compact.
- Surface print constraints honestly: page size, bleed, color mode, font status.
- Let AI actions feel like commands inside a professional tool, not a chatbot bolted onto the side.
